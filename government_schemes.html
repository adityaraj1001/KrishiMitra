Income Support
PM-KISAN
Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi
Direct income support of ₹6,000 per year to all landholding farmer families, released in 3 equal installments of ₹2,000 directly to bank accounts.

Key Benefits

✓
₹6,000/year in 3 installments of ₹2,000 each
✓
Direct Bank Transfer (DBT) to Aadhaar-linked account
✓
All landholding farmer families eligible
✓
No income cap — all small, marginal, large farmers
🗓️ Since Feb 2019 👨‍🌾 14 Crore+ Farmers 💰 ₹6,000/year
🌐 Apply Now 📋 Check Status

Crop Insurance
PMFBY
Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana
Comprehensive crop insurance scheme covering losses from natural calamities, pests, and diseases. Farmer pays very low premium; rest is subsidized by government.

Key Benefits

✓
Premium: 2% for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi, 5% for Horticulture
✓
Full sum insured based on crop area notified
✓
Covers: drought, flood, cyclone, pest, hailstorm, disease
✓
Satellite imagery & tech-based loss assessment
🌾 Kharif + Rabi 📍 Pan India 💰 Low Premium
🌐 Apply Now 📝 Register

Credit & Loan

KCC — Kisan Credit Card Scheme
Provides farmers with affordable credit for crop cultivation, post-harvest expenses, produce marketing, and consumption needs at subsidized interest rates.

Key Benefits

✓
Government subvention: 2% + 3% for timely repayment
✓
No collateral required up to ₹1.6 lakh
✓
Insurance cover for farmer + crop included
💰 4% Interest 🏦 All Banks ✅ Zero Collateral <₹1.6L

Watershed development and groundwater recharge

Pension

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maandhan Yojana

Key Benefits

✓
₹3,000/month pension after age 60
✓
Equal contribution by farmer + Government
✓
₹55–₹200/month farmer contribution (age dependent)
✓
Family pension to spouse on farmer's death
👴 Age 18–40 💰 ₹3,000/month

70–80% loan against stored produce value
✓
Interest subvention: 3% for up to 6 months
✓
Sell when prices are better — no distress sale
✓
Produce insured during storage period
🏬 Warehouse Loan 💰 70–80% Advance
